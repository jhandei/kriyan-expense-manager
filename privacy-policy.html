Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Kriyan Expense Manager ("Kriyan", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Kriyan Expense Manager mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Kriyan does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

All data you enter in the App — including expenses, budgets, categories, and income — is stored locally on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect financial account information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising networks.
  • We do not sell or share your data with any third party.

3. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App may use anonymized analytics and crash reporting tools (such as Amplitude and Sentry) to help us understand how the App is used and to fix technical issues. These tools do not collect personally identifiable information. Any data collected is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you.

4. AI-Powered Insights

Kriyan includes educational spending insights and suggestions. These are generated based on data stored locally on your device and are intended for informational purposes only. Kriyan does not provide financial advice. All insights are educational and non-specific in nature.

5. Children's Privacy

Kriyan is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
